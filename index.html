About Me

My name is Mathume Mahubane, a Bachelor of Oral Health Sciences student at Wits University and a proud Promaths Alumnus. I am passionate about dentistry and public health, with interests in prosthodontics, oral pathology, and orthodontics. My studies are not only a clinical pursuit but also a way to make a meaningful impact on community wellbeing.

My goal is to combine clinical expertise with public health initiatives to create sustainable solutions. I envision designing responsive, user-centered websites and portfolios that showcase professional work while providing accessible education. Ultimately, I hope to contribute to a future where oral health is recognized as essential to overall health and communities are equipped with the knowledge they need to thrive.
